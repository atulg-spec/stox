What is Trading

Profiting from buying and selling assets.

Trading refers to the buying and selling of financial instruments, such as stocks, bonds, commodities, currencies, and derivatives, with the aim of making a profit from the price fluctuations of these assets. Traders engage in trading activities to capitalize on short-term price movements, often taking advantage of both rising and falling prices.
